Qualifications of His Highness Sheikh Doctor Sultan Bin Muhammad Al Qasimi

His Highness Sheikh Doctor Sultan Bin Muhammad Al Qasimi went to Al Eslah Al Qasimia School for his primary education, during 1948.

He later completed his elementary and secondary education in Sharjah and Dubai.

His Bachelor degree was in Agriculture, which he studied in College of Agriculture, Cairo University, which is in Cairo, Egypt, during 1971.

He got his Doctor status with Distinction i n history during 1985, in Exeter University, which is in Exeter, UK. His thesis was on the accusations of piracy and trials of the East India company to control the Gulf, which he did during 1797-1820.

He got his PhD degree in Political Geography of the Gulf, in University of Durham, UK, in 1999.
